Privacy Policy

Little Brain: Words for Kids  ·  com.mkd.littlesteps

Last updated: 12 July 2026
The short version: Little Brain collects no data at all. It has no internet permission, no ads, no accounts, and no tracking — so your child's data can't go anywhere, because the app can't send anything anywhere.

📋 Data we collect

None. Little Brain does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or device data. Specifically, the app:

  • Requires no internet access — it works fully offline and declares no network permission, so it is technically unable to send data anywhere
  • Has no user accounts, sign-up, or login
  • Contains no advertising of any kind
  • Uses no analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs that collect data
  • Requests no device permissions (no camera, microphone, location, contacts, or storage access)
  • Makes no in-app purchases and shows no links that take children outside the app

💾 Data stored on the device

The app saves a small amount of progress information — the selected language, earned stars and badges — only on the device itself. This data never leaves the device, is not linked to any identity, and is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

🔊 Text-to-speech

Word pronunciations use the text-to-speech engine already installed on the device (such as Google Text-to-Speech). The app sends the word to be spoken to the on-device engine only; it does not transmit anything over the internet itself. The device's TTS engine is governed by its own provider's privacy policy.

👶 Children's privacy

Little Brain is designed for children and is built to comply with the Google Play Families Policy, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and the GDPR. Because the app collects no data at all, no personal information from children (or anyone else) is ever gathered or processed.

🔄 Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the "Last updated" date above will change.
