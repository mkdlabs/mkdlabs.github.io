Privacy Policy

Hindu Calendar हिन्दू पंचांग  ·  com.mkd.hinducalendar

Last updated: 16 July 2026
The short version: Hindu Calendar works 100% offline and collects no data at all. Everything you enter — birth details for Kundali, your city for Panchang timings — stays on your phone and is never sent anywhere.

📋 Data we collect

None. Hindu Calendar does not collect, store on our servers, transmit, or share any personal information or device data. Specifically, the app:

  • Works fully offline — every calculation (Panchang, Kundali, Muhurat) happens on your device
  • Has no user accounts, sign-up, or login
  • Contains no advertising of any kind
  • Uses no analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs that collect data
  • Makes no in-app purchases

💾 Data stored on the device

Some features let you enter information, and all of it is stored only on your device:

  • City selection — used to calculate sunrise, sunset, moonrise and moonset for your location; saved locally so the app remembers your choice
  • Kundali details — birth date, time and place you enter to generate a Janam Kundli or Gun Milan; saved kundalis remain on your phone only
  • Preferences — settings such as chart style and ayanamsha

This data is never uploaded, is not linked to any identity, and is deleted when you clear the app's data or uninstall the app.

📄 PDF sharing

You can save a Kundali as a PDF and share it. Sharing is entirely user-initiated — the PDF is created on your device and handed to the app you choose (such as WhatsApp or email). Where you send it is under your control, and the receiving app's own privacy policy applies to anything you share.

🔄 Changes to this policy

If a future version of the app ever changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the "Last updated" date above will change.
